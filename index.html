About the App

A Wild World in Your Pocket

AnimalVerse is a premium interactive iOS app that transforms how kids discover the animal kingdom. Powered by SwiftUI and ARKit, it blends learning with play through a gorgeous "Liquid Glass" design that kids absolutely love.

Explore over 5 animal categories, unlock badges, ace quizzes, and place life-size 3D animals in your own living room — all without any account or personal data required.

🐾 Mammals 🐦 Birds 🦎 Reptiles 🐸 Amphibians 🐟 Fish 🌿 100% Kid-Safe 🔒 No Account Needed
Core Features

Everything Kids Need to Explore

Four powerful experiences — all wrapped in a stunning Liquid Glass interface designed for joy and discovery.

📖

Animal Encyclopedia

Explore hundreds of animals categorized by type. Each entry features scientific name, diet, conservation status, habitat, and fun facts — all in beautifully crafted glass cards.

🥽

AR Experiences

Place life-size 3D animal models in your room and visualize complete food chains in augmented reality. Learning has never felt this real or this magical.

🎮

Gamified Quizzes

Sound Quiz, Word Jumble, and Category Quiz keep kids engaged and learning. Identify animals by their real calls, unscramble names, and test your knowledge of animal traits.

🏆

Badges & Progress

A persistent badge system rewards exploration and quiz completions. Progress is saved locally — no account needed. The more you explore, the more you earn!

🎨

Liquid Glass Design

Built with glassmorphism and full Dark Mode support. Dynamic color themes for each category, animated mesh backgrounds, and confetti celebrations for achievements.

🥽 ARKit + RealityKit

See Animals Come Alive in Your World

Place a full-scale elephant in your living room or build a complete food chain on your kitchen table. AnimalVerse's AR experiences make the animal kingdom tangible and unforgettable.

AnimalVerse · Last updated: April 7, 2026

Introduction

AnimalVerse ("the App") is designed to help children explore and learn about the animal kingdom in a safe, interactive environment. Your privacy matters deeply to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account creation is required
  • No personal information is requested
  • No location data is collected
  • No contact information is collected
  • No device identifiers are stored or transmitted
  • No analytics or third-party tracking tools are used

Local Data Storage

The App may store certain information locally on your device only, such as explored animals, quiz scores, badge progress, and user preferences. This data remains entirely on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Permissions Usage

The App may request access to certain device features, such as the camera, to enable augmented reality (AR) experiences. These features are used only for app functionality and no data is collected, stored, or transmitted.

Data Transmission

The App does not transmit any data over the internet. All functionality works locally on the device.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services of any kind.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected whatsoever, the App is entirely safe for use by children of all ages. Parents and guardians can feel confident that the App does not collect any personal data. If you have any concerns, you may contact us at the email below.
